Hey there!

I am Priscilla Carlsson, a UX-designer based in Stockholm.

I like to be creative through dance, photography and interior design. Whenever I get the chance I travel to experience a new adventure, or I'll listen to a podcast to learn something new about the human psyche.

My inner artist and psychologist go hand in hand in my work as a UX-designer, with some frontend-knowledge as the icing on the cake. It all started during my studies for a bachelor-degree that introduced me to Design Thinking and later led me in to the world of UX. What gets me excited about it is the user-centered way to solve the problem, and making more than just a pretty front. To actually make a difference through the experience that people have with digital prodcuts every day.
